### [Sky Striker](sky-striker/)
> _A PoC 2D aircraft shooter built with Bevy + Rust._

An early prototype: a pixelated sky shooter with basic enemy waves, shooting mechanics, and score tracking. Play it live: [sky-striker/](sky-striker/)

### [Terra Deck](terra-deck/)
> _A PoC turn-based card RPG built with Bevy + Rust._

An early prototype: a deck-based combat experiment where each suit represents a distinct combat archetype, rendered on a 2D canvas. Play it live: [terra-deck/](terra-deck/)

A lemonade stand empire builder: click to sell glasses, hire helpers, buy upgrades, unlock achievements, and prestige for permanent bonuses. Play it live: [lemonade-empire/](lemonade-empire/)

Auto-fire, survive endless waves, unlock permanent upgrades. A turret defense experiment with progression systems and persistent bonuses. Play it live: [turret-survivor/](turret-survivor/)
